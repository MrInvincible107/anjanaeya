Our Vision

Home is a feeling. And our mission is to preserve life's most special moments. Anjaneya Experience Home Stay is more than just a place to stay; it’s an invitation to experience the simple joys of life in the lap of nature. Located in Sitla, Mukteshwar, Uttarakhand, our property has been lovingly curated to offer guests a peaceful and authentic experience, complete with modern comforts. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or a longer retreat, we aim to make your stay memorable with personalized services and experiences that reflect the true spirit of the region.

Every detail at Anjaneya is thoughtfully curated to enrich our guests’ experiences. From serene surroundings to personalized services, we strive to create moments you will cherish long after your stay.
